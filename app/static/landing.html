Explore the connections between your ideas.

People don't think in paragraphs. Thinktool lets you write down what you're thinking in the way you're thinking it.

Start by writing down everything as a series of bullet points. Then you can add connections between different items, organize them into hierarchies and even define complex relationships between them.

Then, explore. You can see your ideas laid out in a hierarchy, jump from one thought to the next, or create a custom view to see your ideas in the way that is the most useful to you.

In the process of transforming knowledge from a static chunk of text into a detailed, interactive system of individual ideas, you will gain a deeper understanding of what you already know.
